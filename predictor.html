How this was built

  • Trained on 17,525 cleaned survey responses using linear regression (chosen over a random forest, which performed marginally worse on this data).
  • Inputs: years of experience, country, and education level — one-hot encoded.
  • 6 countries with extremely large, unstable coefficients (likely from having only 1–2 respondents each) were excluded from this tool to avoid nonsensical estimates.
  • Every prediction is just: base rate + (years × experience weight) + country adjustment + education adjustment — the breakdown above shows each piece explicitly, nothing hidden.
  • This runs entirely in your browser — no server, no data leaves your device.
